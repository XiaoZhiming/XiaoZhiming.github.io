Trino源码学习 Client

还是以TpchQueryRunner创建的client为例，其它client（命令行client，启动类io.trino.cli.trion）处理query的流程也类似

提交查询

sequenceDiagram
StatementClientV1->>QueuedStatementResource:/v1/statement
 postStatement 
note over QueuedStatementResource,queryManager: 注册query
QueuedStatementResource->>queryManager:registerQuery
QueuedStatementResource-->>StatementClientV1:QueryResults [nextUri=/v1/statement/queued]
query还未提交coordinatorLocation不存在
StatementClientV1->>QueuedStatementResource:/v1/statement/queued
 getStatus
note over QueuedStatementResource: 提交query
QueuedStatementResource-->>StatementClientV1:QueryResults [nextUri=/v1/statement/queued]
query还未提交完成coordinatorLocation不存在
client重新请求/v1/statement/queued
QueuedStatementResource-->>StatementClientV1:QueryResults [nextUri=/v1/statement/executing]
query提交完成coordinatorLocation存在
loop 获取执行状态、结果直到完成
StatementClientV1->>ExecutingStatementResource:/v1/statement/executing
getQueryResults
ExecutingStatementResource-->>StatementClientV1:QueryResults
end
